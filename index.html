About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), working on multilingual data curation, NLP, and interdisciplinary projects. My research spans over 15 years of teaching and research in AI, Machine Learning, and Sentiment Analysis, with a strong focus on socially relevant applications. I received the Best Thesis Award (2018) from IEEE Bangalore Section for my doctoral work and was a Gold Medalist in my Master's program.

Experience

  • Professor and HOD (CSE), KSSEM, Bangalore, India (2020–2022) – Lead the department with all the managerial activities and coordination between students, faculties and directors
  • Associate Professor (CSE), KSSEM, Bangalore, India (2018–2020) – taught ML, AI, Python, Data Mining, and mentored research projects
  • Assistant Professor (CSE), TJIT, Bangalore, India (2012–2018) – taught Data Mining, Software Engineering and handled labs
  • Lecturer (CSE), GEIT, Dehradun, India (2003–2012) – Handled labs, time-table coordinator, exam coordinator
